About Abay Taxi

At Abay Taxi, we pride ourselves on being Fort McMurray’s most reliable and user-friendly transportation service. Whether you're commuting for work, heading to a social event, or exploring the city, we connect you with safe, trusted drivers who are always ready to get you where you need to go. Our platform is designed with ease of use in mind, offering quick booking, live taxi tracking, and a seamless experience for every rider. At Abay Taxi, your safety, comfort, and convenience are our top priorities, ensuring you reach your destination with peace of mind. Join us as we drive Fort McMurray forward, one ride at a time!

Your well-being is our top priority.



Our Mission

At Abay Taxi, our mission is to revolutionize transportation in Fort McMurray by offering a reliable, safe, and seamless travel experience for everyone. We aim to connect riders with trusted drivers through our easy-to-use platform, ensuring convenience at every turn. Whether it’s a daily commute or a special occasion, our goal is to provide an exceptional service that prioritizes customer satisfaction, safety, and comfort. With innovation and community at the heart of everything we do, we are committed to driving positive change and enhancing mobility for all.

Our Story

Abay Taxi was founded with a vision to transform transportation in Fort McMurray. What began as a small initiative to meet the growing demand for reliable, safe, and efficient taxi services has evolved into a trusted platform that connects riders with dedicated drivers. From day one, our focus has been on providing a smooth and seamless travel experience, blending cutting-edge technology with a deep understanding of our community’s needs. As we’ve grown, so has our commitment to innovation and excellence, ensuring that every ride is not just a trip, but a service built on trust and convenience. Abay Taxi continues to be driven by our passion for offering the best taxi experience, making every journey a meaningful part of our story.
